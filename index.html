About Me

Before I became a UI/UX Designer, I was a Software Engineering undergraduate with a growing curiosity about how people experience technology.

My journey into UI/UX design began about one and a half years ago, and what I’ve discovered is that great design is about more than just aesthetics. It’s about empathy, problem solving, and making someone’s day just a little bit easier.

Since then, I’ve worked as a UI/UX Designer, diving into user research, wireframes, prototyping, and creating clean, intuitive interfaces. What keeps me motivated is knowing that behind every screen is a real person, and great design can make their day just a little easier.

Moving forward, I’m excited to keep learning, growing, and collaborating with others to create digital experiences that truly matter.
